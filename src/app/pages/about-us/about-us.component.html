Who are We?

Check DBS is a DBS provider offering fast, reliable, and hassle-free criminal background checks for businesses and individuals. Trading under Cloudnex-UK Ltd, we are a modern, customer focused start-up dedicated to simplifying the DBS process while ensuring full compliance with safeguarding standards. What makes us different is our customer-first approach and commitment to quality service at every stage. We understand that background checks are not just about ticking boxes – they are about trust, clarity, and peace of mind. We go the extra mile to offer unmatched after-check support, assisting customers with registration for the DBS Update Service and DBS Online Account, and guiding them through any post-check queries.

Our team includes professionals with direct experience working within the Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS). With robust in-house IT support, we deliver a smooth and secure experience from start to finish. In addition to DBS checks, we also offer Right to Work Checks and Digital Identity Checks, providing a complete solution for your employment and compliance needs.
