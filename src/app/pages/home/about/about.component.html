Who are We?

Check DBS is a modern, customer-focused platform offering fast and hassle-free criminal background checks for both individuals and businesses. Trading under Cloudnex-UK Ltd, we combine real experience from professionals who've worked directly with the Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) and powerful in-house tech to streamline the entire process. We’re more than just a DBS provider — we offer ongoing support, help with the DBS Update Service, online account setup, and guidance on post-check queries. Whether you need a single check or manage checks for a team, we’re here to make it simple, secure, and stress-free.
